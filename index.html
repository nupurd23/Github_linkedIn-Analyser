About SkillScan360

SkillScan360 is a comprehensive platform designed to analyze and compare professional profiles from LinkedIn and GitHub. Our advanced analytics provide deep insights into skills, experience, and career progression.

Features

📊

Detailed Analytics

Get comprehensive insights into profile statistics, skills distribution, and activity patterns.

🔍

Profile Comparison

Compare two profiles side-by-side to identify strengths, gaps, and opportunities.

📈

Visual Reports

Interactive charts and graphs make it easy to understand complex data patterns.

🎯

Skill Assessment

Identify key skills and technologies based on project history and endorsements.

How It Works

Simply enter a LinkedIn or GitHub username, and our system will analyze the public profile data to generate comprehensive reports. Our algorithms extract meaningful insights from profile information, project repositories, connections, and activity patterns.

Whether you're a recruiter looking to assess candidates, a professional wanting to optimize your profile, or simply curious about industry trends, SkillScan360 provides the tools you need.
