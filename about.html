What is SkillScan360?

SkillScan360 is a powerful tool designed to analyze your GitHub and LinkedIn profiles to help you understand your public presence and technical contributions. Whether you're a student, developer, or job seeker, our analyzer gives valuable insights and AI-generated suggestions for improvement.

Why Use It?

We combine machine learning with modern UI to help you:

  • Evaluate your GitHub activity visually
  • Compare multiple developer profiles
  • Get suggestions for improving your online presence
  • Export your insights as shareable reports
